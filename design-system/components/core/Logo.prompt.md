The nestia. wordmark lockup — Cormorant Garamond with the petal-rouge period and italic tagline.

```jsx
<Logo size={48} />
<Logo tone="cream" size={32} />            {/* on dark surfaces */}
<Logo tone="ink" showTagline={false} />    {/* compact, no tagline */}
```

`tone`: `plum` (default) · `cream` (dark surfaces) · `ink`. `size` is the wordmark px; the tagline scales from it. The period is always Petal Rouge.
