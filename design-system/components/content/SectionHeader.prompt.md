Standard section opener: eyebrow, title, petal-dot divider, and a lead line.

```jsx
<SectionHeader
  eyebrow="Menu"
  title="人気のメニュー"
  lead="その日の心と体に合わせて、ゆっくりと整えていきます。"
/>
```

`titleFont`: `serif-jp` (default mincho) or `display` (Latin Cormorant). `align`: `center` (default) or `left`. `tone="dark"` for plum sections. `divider={false}` to hide the petal rule.
