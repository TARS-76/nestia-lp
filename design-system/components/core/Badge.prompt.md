Pill label for a category, menu name, or small status mark.

```jsx
<Badge variant="plum">オーダーメイドケア</Badge>
<Badge>人気No.1</Badge>
<Badge variant="accent">本日空きあり</Badge>
```

Variants: `soft` (almond, default) · `plum` (solid — the flyer's menu-name pills) · `outline` · `accent` (petal wash, urgency). Sizes `sm | md | lg`.
